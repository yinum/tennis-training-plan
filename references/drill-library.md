# Drill library — by rubric dimension, 3 levels, equipment variants

Levels map to mesocycles: L1 acquisition (low pressure, blocked), L2 consolidation
(time pressure, variable), L3 integration (match conditions, random). Equipment
tags: [wall] [machine] [partner] [coach] [solo]. Pick the variant the player's
access allows; every dimension has at least one [solo]/[wall] option so no plan
dies for lack of a partner. Attach a measurable success metric to every drill —
suggested metrics included; scale counts to the player's level.

## Serve
- **L1 Toss consistency** [solo]: toss into a racquet target on the ground; 15/20 within the zone. Trophy-freeze checks in mirror/video.
- **L1 Knee-bend serve** [solo]: serve with exaggerated bend-hold-drive count ("down-up-hit"); video 10 serves, knee angle visibly <150° on 8.
- **L2 Zone serving** [solo]: cones deuce/ad corners; 6/10 first serves to a called zone at 80% effort.
- **L2 Second-serve spin ladder** [solo]: continental spin serves from service line → 3/4 court → baseline; advance at 7/10 in.
- **L3 Serve +1 pattern** [partner/machine]: serve wide, next ball to open court; win the +1 exchange 6/10. Pressure sets: two second serves only, play to 7.

## Return
- **L1 Split-step timing** [wall/partner]: split as feeder hits; video check 10/10 splits present.
- **L2 Block returns** [machine/partner]: compact block vs pace to deep middle; 7/10 past service line.
- **L3 Return games** [partner]: return-only games, second-serve returns taken inside baseline; win return point ≥40%.

## Forehand
- **L1 Spacing drop-feeds** [solo/coach]: drop-feed, contact in front checkpoint, hold finish 2s; 15/20 clean with balance held.
- **L1 Unit-turn shadow** [solo]: shadow with resistance band or racquet-throat hold; 3×10 with hip-shoulder separation visible on video.
- **L2 Figure-8 rally** [partner/machine]: alternate cross/line on the move; 20-ball streak with depth past service line.
- **L3 Forehand hunt** [partner]: baseline points where only forehand winners count double; run-around forehand on any middle ball.

## Backhand
- **L1 Contact-height ladder** [machine/partner]: feeds at knee/waist/shoulder; 12/15 clean at each height before advancing.
- **L2 Cross-court grind** [partner/wall]: backhand cross only, 25-ball rally target with margin >1m over net.
- **L2 Slice knife** [partner]: alternate drive/slice; slice must land past service line 7/10, bouncing below knee.
- **L3 Breakdown defense** [partner]: opponent attacks backhand at will; survive 6-ball attack sequences 5/10 without a short ball.

## Net Game
- **L1 Volley wall** [wall]: continental punch volleys, 30 consecutive; no backswing past shoulder on video.
- **L2 Approach-volley-overhead** [machine/partner/coach]: short ball → approach → volley → lob → overhead circuit; 6/10 sequences finished.
- **L3 Attack ladder** [partner]: any ball landing short of service line must be approached; win ≥50% of approach points.

## Movement & Footwork
- **L1 Split-step metronome** [solo]: shadow rally with split on every imaginary opponent hit, 2×2 min; video: zero missed splits in final 30s.
- **L1 Ladder/cone patterns** [solo]: side shuffle, crossover recovery, X-drill; time-boxed 10 min.
- **L2 Figure-8 baseline** [solo/partner]: corner-to-corner with recovery to bisector, 8 balls/rep, 6 reps; balance held at each shadow contact.
- **L3 Defense-to-offense points** [partner]: start each point pulled wide from a feed; recover and win ≥40%.

## Rally Consistency
- **L1 Wall tempo** [wall]: fixed-tempo rally, 50 consecutive; **L2**: 30 with alternating targets.
- **L2 Depth discipline** [partner/machine]: rope/line 2m inside baseline; 15-ball rallies where 80% land beyond it.
- **L3 First-to-20** [partner]: cooperative-to-competitive ladder — 10 co-op balls then point plays; unforced-error count logged per set of 20.

## Power & Spin
- **L1 Racquet-head-speed shadow** [solo]: overspeed shadow swings (lighter racquet/towel snap), 3×8.
- **L2 Spin windows** [partner/machine]: hit through a 1–2 m window above net with visible dip; 7/10.
- **L3 Green-light balls** [partner]: any ball landing mid-court gets full-pace attack; keep attack error rate <30%.

## Tactics & Shot Selection
- **L1 Pattern walkthrough** [solo]: annotate own match video — mark every direction change as right/wrong time; ≥70% right next review.
- **L2 Constraint games** [partner]: cross-court-only until short ball; serve+1 to a called zone; win by executing the pattern, not the exception.
- **L3 Scouting sets** [partner]: play sets with one written game plan (e.g., "attack the backhand, approach on anything short"); self-grade adherence ≥80%.

## Competitive Habits
- **L1 Routine build** [solo]: scripted between-point routine (breath → racquet strings → next-point cue); rehearse in practice games.
- **L2 Scoreboard pressure** [partner]: every practice game starts 30-30; log double faults and rushed errors.
- **L3 Match log** [solo]: after each match, 3 lines — what won points, what lost points, one adjustment made mid-match. Review at week's end.

## Fitness / injury-prevention micro-doses (append to any session)
- Shoulder: band external rotations 2×15 before serve days.
- Knees/ankles: lateral bounds + single-leg balance 5 min.
- Core anti-rotation (pallof press) 2×12 — supports the unit turn.
- These are maintenance doses, not a fitness program; refer out for real S&C.
